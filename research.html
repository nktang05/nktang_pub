Research

My research centers on global health analytics, environmental exposure, and data-driven interventions to promote equitable access to care and inform evidence-based policy.

Better Health Starts with Her — Kibera, Kenya

Community-led women’s health study focused on reproductive health, youth engagement, and clinical access in informal settlements.

  • Presented at APRU Global Health Conference, Bangkok 2024.
  • Led workshops for 60+ local Youth Public Health Ambassadors.
  • Designed survey structure, data cleaning scripts, and SQL-based health dashboards.

Understanding Road Trauma and Injury — Lira, Uganda

Field-based study integrating infrastructure, behavior, and health outcomes to reduce road trauma and injury rates in East Africa.

  • Presented at CUGH 2025, Atlanta.
  • Developed survey and data collection methods; conducted descriptive analysis in R and Tableau.
  • Produced a policy brief summarizing findings for the Ugandan Ministry of Health.
