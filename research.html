Research Projects

Burn Occurrence and Care in a Ugandan Informal Urban Settlement: A Qualitative Exploration

Sub-Saharan Africa has the highest incidence of burns per capita. Burn prevalence is particularly high in informal urban settlements where risk factors converge. This study provides new qualitative evidence to identify burn injury risk factors and characterize barriers to care which prevent the effective prevention and treatment of burn injuries.

  • Conducted interviews and focus group to evaluate causes of burns, treatment option misconceptions, and treatment-seeking behavior
